About the App

Sparrow is a cutting-edge campus navigation application designed to help students, faculty, and visitors navigate your campus with ease. Using advanced mapping technology and an intuitive interface, Sparrow makes finding your way around campus effortless.

How to Use

Open the Map

Access the interactive campus map to see your location and all points of interest

Search Locations

Use the search feature to quickly find specific buildings, labs, cafeterias, or parking areas

Get Directions

Receive step-by-step walking directions to any location on campus
